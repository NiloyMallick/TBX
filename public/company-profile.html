TBX Group - Company Profile

Executive Summary

TBX Group is a vertically integrated apparel sourcing and supply chain conglomerate delivering excellence to the global fashion industry. With a foundation built on the factory floor, we have evolved into a comprehensive solution provider, bridging the gap between design and delivery through our specialized subsidiaries.

Core Values:

  • Building Partnerships: Long-term collaboration with global brands.
  • Excellence in Manufacturing: State-of-the-art facilities ensuring precision.
  • Sustainability: Commitment to ethical sourcing and eco-friendly practices.
