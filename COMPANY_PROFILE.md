# TBX Group - Company Profile

## Executive Summary
TBX Group is a vertically integrated apparel sourcing and supply chain conglomerate delivering excellence to the global fashion industry. With a foundation built on the factory floor, we have evolved into a comprehensive solution provider, bridging the gap between design and delivery through our specialized subsidiaries.

**Core Values:**
- **Building Partnerships:** Long-term collaboration with global brands.
- **Excellence in Manufacturing:** State-of-the-art facilities ensuring precision.
- **Sustainability:** Commitment to ethical sourcing and eco-friendly practices.

### 2. A&A Fashion & Fabrics
*Leading 100% Export-Oriented Knit Fabrics Manufacturer*

**Overview:**
A&A Fashion & Fabrics supplies premium-quality knitted fabrics to global apparel brands, buying houses, and garment factories. We focus on superior fabric quality, consistency, and on-time production.

**Management:**
- **Sadekuzzaman** - Managing Director
- **Sonia Afrin** - Director

**Factory Capabilities:**
- **Total Area:** 18,700 sq. ft.
- **Daily Capacity:** 5,000–6,000 kg of knit fabrics
- **Facilities:** Modern production floor, natural lighting, skilled workforce.

**Product Range:**
- Single Jersey, Rib & Interlock
- Fleece (1, 2, 3-thread), French Terry
- Lycra & Spandex Fabrics
- Pique, Lacoste, Waffle, Cable Knit
- Mélange, Cotton-Modal, Indigo Dyed
- Auto Stripes, Jacquard, AOP
